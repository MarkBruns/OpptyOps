# 20 Categories of Robotic-Focused Content

### 1. Humanoid Robots
Humanoid robots seek to mimic human form and dexterity to perform versatile tasks in shared human environments, from factories to homes. In the last two years, major players demonstrated impressive AI-driven autonomy, with Figure AI releasing its Helix vision-language-action model, Ubtech delivering over 500 industrial units, and pilots at sites like BMW factories alongside Tesla Optimus and Boston Dynamics’ electric Atlas prototypes gaining traction through massive funding rounds exceeding $1B each.

The category carries risks of over-excitement, as critics like Rodney Brooks label flashy demos “humanoid theater” — prototypes often remain costly, energy-hungry, and unreliable in unstructured settings, with commercialization timelines repeatedly slipping despite investor hype and record valuations.
