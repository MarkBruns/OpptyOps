### Phase 3: Interview & Offer Phase — Relentless Execution
Typical Anduril process (recruiter screen → technical → hiring manager → onsite/virtual loop with cross-functional team):
- **Recruiter screen**: Bring Mastery Map. Ask: “What are the top 2–3 material-flow bottlenecks the team is facing right now in Arsenal-1 ramp?”
- **Technical rounds**: Walk through your simulation demo live. Be ready for gemba-style questions (“Walk me through how you would fix a parts-shortage bottleneck on the floor tomorrow”).
- **Behavioral / culture fit**: Every answer ties back to “dogfooding the role,” speed of iteration, service to the warfighter via better material flow.
- **Final round**: Present my full deck + 90-day plan.

**Follow-up rule**: Within 24 hours of every interview, send a 1-page “Value Delivered Already” note + one new insight or improvement idea based on what I have learned in the interview.
